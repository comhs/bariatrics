About Community Healthcare System Bariatric Surgery

Community Healthcare System is the area’s most experienced and comprehensive destination for bariatric surgery and long-term weight loss. Our patients have access to the full resources of a highly trained team focused on the mental, physical and nutritional support needed to help people reach their weight-loss goals, keep the weight off and live healthier lives.

Since 2000, our accredited program has been led by board certified bariatric surgeons who have performed more surgeries than anyone in Northwest Indiana. Our team is dedicated to helping people get their lives back, so they can live their life to the fullest. We’ve performed over 2,000 successful surgeries and have reduced the risks associated with diabetes, hypertension, sleep apnea, coronary artery disease and other health complications.
